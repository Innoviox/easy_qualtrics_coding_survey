You will be labeling brief descriptions of people's dining experiences and labeling them as positive or negative.

 

Descriptions indicating a positive dining experience might mention things like mentioning:
  • Delicious and well-prepared food
  • Attentive and friendly service
  • Comfortable and inviting ambiance
  • Convenient location
  • Good value for the price

 

Descriptions indicating a negative dining experience might mention things like:

 

  • Dirty or poorly maintained dining area
  • Overpriced menu items
  • Unfriendly or rude staff
  • Limited or uninspiring menu options
  • Issues with reservations or seating arrangements
